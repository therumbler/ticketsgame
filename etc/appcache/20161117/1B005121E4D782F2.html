    American Express Preferred Seating
    All eligible Card members have exclusive access to the most sought-after tickets for some of the hottest shows across the country, in some cases before they're available to the general public.

    The following American Express Card members are eligible for this benefit:
    Domestic and International: Gold Card, Platinum Card and Centurion Card; International Dollar Cards; Corporate Platinum Card and the Executive Business Card; Corporate Centurion Card; Business Gold Card; Business Platinum Card and Business Centurion Card from OPEN: The Small Business Network, if issued by American Express Travel Related Services Company Inc.; American Express Centurion Bank; American Express Bank, FSB; Platinum Delta SkyMiles Credit Card; Delta Reserve Credit Card from American Express, Platinum Delta SkyMiles Business Credit Card; and Delta Reserve for Business Credit Card from American Express

    To purchase your American Express Preferred Seating tickets online:
    1. In the "Enter offer code/password" prompt, if you are a Domestic Card member, enter the 10-digit Customer Service phone number located on the back of your Card. If you are an International Card member, enter your promotional code.
    2. Click OK to apply your password and browse preferred seats directly on the seat map.
    3. To complete your purchase, click on your desired seats and proceed to Checkout.
    1. Domestic Cardmembers, enter the 10-digit Customer Service phone number on the back of your card. International Cardmembers, enter your promotional code.
    2. Click "Enter" to unlock the offer.
    3. Click "Get Tickets" to continue.

    Please note that the tickets available through the American Express Preferred Seating program are available exclusively for qualifying Card members at no additional cost. Standard Ticketmaster service charges apply. American Express Preferred Seating tickets are subject to availability and apply to select events.

    If you are a Domestic Card member, for more information on Preferred Seating, please call 1-800-448-TIKS or visit American Express. If you are an International Card member, please call the customer service number located on the back of your Card. International inquires will not be serviced on the U.S. 800 number listed above.
